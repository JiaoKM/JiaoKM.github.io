Biography

I'm looking for summer internship opportunities 2025 in EU and part-time jobs in Lund/Malmö, Sweden!
I'm a master student in LTH, Lund University, enrolled in the Master's Programme in Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality. Before that, I got my bachelor's degree at School of Electronic Information & Electrical Engineering (SEIEE), Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), majoring in Electronic Science & Technology. Now I'm interested in Computer Graphics, VR&AR and Computer Vision. I hope I can make my own contribution in these areas.

On my student ID card, I am an engineering student. In my mind, I am a society observer. But in my heart, I am just a lifelong gamer exploring the world.
